Learning Record Store

Whatever you're doing with Tin Can, you'll need a Learning Record Store (LRS) to store your learning data. The LRS can sit within a Learner Management System (LMS), or can be a separate application outside the LMS. If your LMS doesn't already contain an LRS, you'll need to get one.

Choosing an LRS can be a complex decision. Should you use a cloud based or hosted solution? An off-the-shelf product or a bespoke development? Epic (the company I work for) are experts in learning platforms, including LRS. We can help you to work out the best solution for your organisation and then either build your solution or direct you to off-the-shelf products as appropriate.
