Learning Record Store

Whatever you're doing with Tin Can, you'll need a Learning Record Store (LRS) to store your learning data. The LRS can sit within a Learner Management System (LMS), or can be a separate application outside the LMS. If your LMS doesn't already contain an LRS, you'll need to get one.

I'm on the technical board of open source LRS Learning Locker. If you need any help getting started with Learning Locker or customising it to meet your needs, I'd love to hear from you.

Find out more about Epic's LRS services, and then get in touch.
